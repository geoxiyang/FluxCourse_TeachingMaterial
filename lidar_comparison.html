Time-of-Flight

Range: up to 300m+ outdoor
Precision: ~1–5 cm (timer-limited)
Simple hardware — just one fast timer
Used in: autonomous vehicles, SLAM

Phase-Shift

Unambiguous range: 100 m at 1.5 MHz
Precision: sub-mm to ~1mm
Multi-frequency resolves ambiguity
Used in: 3D scanning, BIM, metrology
