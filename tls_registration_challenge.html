Scan Registration Challenge

Two TLS scans of the same forest plot, captured from different positions. They share the same coordinate space conceptually — but aren't aligned yet.

Find the spherical targets in each scan. Click a target in Scan A, then click the matching target in Scan B to form a pair. You need at least 3 pairs.

3
Apply transform to Scan B, update RMSE
4
